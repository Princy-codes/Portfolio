Welcome to the Climate Change Impact Visualizer, an interactive platform dedicated to raising awareness about the pressing issues of climate change.

This project is built using modern web technologies including React.js, Node.js, and MongoDB, with integrations of various APIs for real-time data visualization.

"Empowering individuals with data-driven insights to understand and combat climate change."

Our platform offers dynamic maps, interactive charts, and predictive models to visualize the impacts of climate change across different regions and timeframes. Users can explore real-time climate data, access the latest news on environmental issues, and delve into a repository of research papers and educational resources.

We believe that informed individuals can make a significant difference. By providing accessible and comprehensive climate data, we aim to foster a community that is knowledgeable and proactive in addressing environmental challenges.


    Some key highlights of my project:

  • Interactive Visualizations: Dynamic maps and charts that depict climate trends and projections.

  • Real-Time Data Integration: Live climate statistics and news via integrated APIs.

  • Educational Resources: Curated research papers and informative content for learners and researchers.

  • User-Friendly Interface: Clean and accessible design, suitable for all users.
